Roblox

Implementations

I have done alot of work in Roblox. It all started from making games in Roblox Studio. But it soon grew to much more. I started learning how to integrate roblox with other websites. My first main project was integrating Discord with Roblox to make a cross platform chat. Then I moved on to work with a group of developers to make a game, which did not turn out very well. After that I started to work on the commercial side of Roblox. This is truly the point where I learnt the deep workings of websockets, as well as backends. I have made automated fulfillment solutions for multiple roblox games. These solutions allow for the buyer to list the item on shopify, and run a roblox bot and express backend on a VPS. The backend handles the linking between Shopify and the bot through websockets, while the bot physically interacts with Roblox and gives the buyer his/her items. Most of the codes have been lost to time, or I do not have permission to publically share. I have however uploaded a video of one of the automated fulfillment solutions in the demo.
